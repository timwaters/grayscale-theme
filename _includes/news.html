Call for Proposals Open!

The call for proposals is now open. You have until May 20th to submit your ideas for this years Congress. To give us your proposal, please visit and enter in the details via this Google Form.

For more inspiration, you can look at previous years Congresses including watching the videos of the sessions from 2017, and listen to the RTE Radio show about the Congress

We'd also recommend you signing up to the low volume mailing list for the Congress to get notified of any updates to the call, announcements of the programme and general news about the Congress.
